Dogfish

Size: Exceptionally 6lb or more, UK shore caught typically 1–2lb UK minimum size: 38cm in length. UK shore caught record: 4lb. Extremely unfussy scavenger that will eat pretty much anything it can find. Worms, fish, prawns, shellfish and crustaceans will all be taken, as will small fish it can hunt down.

Bass

Size: up to 4ft and 20lb. UK shore caught typically 1 – 5lb UK minimum size: 42cm in length. Catch and release only in January, February and December of 2020. Anglers limited to retaining two bass per day in all other months. Primarily hunts small fish but will also take worms and crustaceans.

Mackerel

Size: Up to 18 inches in length but typically smaller. UK shore caught record: 1lb 15oz . Feeds on: Mostly smaller fish and small squid, although will also eat prawns and small crustaceans as well.

Garfish

Size: up to 4ft and 50lb+, UK shore caught typically 2 – 10lb Feeds on: Mostly other fish such as herring, sandeel and small mackerel. Coalfish will also feed on the seabed for crustaceans and marine worms.

Pollock

Size: Up to 3ft and 4lbs UK minimum size: 38cm/15inches Feeds on: Small fish such as herring, sprats, sandeels and immature fry of larger species.

Coalfish

Size: up to 4ft and 50lb+, UK shore caught typically 2 – 10lb. Feeds on: Mostly other fish such as herring, sandeel and small mackerel. Coalfish will also feed on the seabed for crustaceans and marine worms.
